§ SPECIMEN TEXT — 正文样张

On the night of the 14th, a fog thick as printer's ink settled over the Strand, and every gas lamp burned like a halftone dot against the dark. The compositor set his type by feel — 1926 had been a year of misregistered fortunes, each page slightly off, and all the more honest for it.

凡本铺印品，须经得起复印机的考验：不靠渐变，不靠辉光，只靠网点、油墨与留白。按钮是铅字块，徽章是火漆印，通知是电报——深夜的黑暗模式，不过是打烊后点起煤气灯的印刷铺。
